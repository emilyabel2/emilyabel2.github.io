Website Evaluation

This is my Website Evaluation Page. Here I will evaluate two websites, one of high quality, and the other of poor quality.

High Quality Website: LoL Patch Notes

The purpose of this website is to display the changes made to this online game in an interesting and engaging way.

    CRAP Evaluation:
  • Contrast - The contrast on this page is very well exectued, with the background being a dark blue and the text being a white with yellow headings. Some links, like the ones in the righthand nav bar are apparent with a nice text decoration when hoverd.
  • Repitition - The repitition of this page is highly evident. There are four core colors, (dark blue, yellow, white, and gray) which repeat all the way down the page. Key information is displayed with a border to prevent confusion between elements. Font and formatting remains the same throughout the entireity of the page.
  • Alignment - Alignment is my largest vice with this page, due to the righthand menu bar being just a few pixels too low (the top menu blocks part of the right menu) Besides that small issue, spacing is consistent throughout the page and within the elements.
  • Proximity - Proximity is perfectly exectued, with appropriate margins between borders edges and text.
